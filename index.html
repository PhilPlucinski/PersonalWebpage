About

"That's me" image found on My-ponchoboys's Tumblr Page

Hi!

My name is Phil Plucinski and I am an aspiring software developer.


I'm currently studying Computer Science at Rutgers University - New Brunswick. I expect to graduate with the Rutgers University Honors College Class of 2019.

At the moment, my strongest languages are Java and C. Although I have dabbled/am dabbling in HTML/CSS, Javascript, JQuery, PHP, C#, and Python.

When I'm not studying or coding, you can find me obsessing over my dog Sasha, eating good food with friends, hitting the gym (at least that's the hope), or just driving around.
